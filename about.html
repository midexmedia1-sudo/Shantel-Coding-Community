About Shantel Coding Community

Shantel Coding Community is more than a tech hub, it is a movement dedicated to transforming lives through innovation, skills, and digital empowerment in Oyo Town and beyond.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bridge the digital divide, Shantel Coding Community began as a small group of passionate learners. Over time, it has grown into a thriving ecosystem of coders, entrepreneurs, and innovators working together to build impactful solutions for society.

From humble beginnings, we have empowered hundreds of young people to explore careers in technology and entrepreneurship, creating job opportunities and startups that strengthen our community.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower individuals with relevant digital skills, foster innovation, and provide opportunities for sustainable growth in technology and business.

Vision

To become the leading innovation hub in Nigeria, where ideas thrive, businesses are born, and communities are transformed through technology.
